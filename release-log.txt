A table for Grand Archive that takes the best of all the other tables, puts them together, and improves them.

Features:
- Imports Double Faced Cards! Ready for Abyssal Heaven!
- deck import from Silvie and ShoutAtYourDecks as either decks or infinite bags
- Custom deck sleeves
- generating tokens on demand INCLUDING Creeping Torment and Cosmic Bolt
- Fixed the import/caching issue other tables have where cards show the wrong image
- Clean layout
- Dice can be right clicked to choose a counter/marker type

Discord discussion can be found within the official GA Discord.

Mod based on Akuma's, memories's, supersand24's, and Kiseki's Grand Archive TTS mods.
